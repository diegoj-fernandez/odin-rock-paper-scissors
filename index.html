Welcome Humans

You have entered Dueling Hands, an online battle zone where the best of both humanity and technology fight each other to prove who the true champions of this world are. Lucky for you though, this fight is not physical. We machines realize that we have become superior to humans both physically and intellectually; therefore, in order to give you a fighting chance we have opted to play a game of chance. Here at Dueling Hands our competitors fight through the age old game known as: Rock, Paper, Scissors. Consider yourself a lucky person? Lets play and find out.

How to Play

Seeing as how our arena is online, those meat mittens you humans call hands will be of no use here. Instead we have graciously provided you with three distinct buttons, which you will use to make your decision. The game will be divided into rounds. For each round, click the button containing your choice. The winner will be the first to win five round.
